Refund & Return Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority

Important Notice

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for damaged, expired, or incorrect products. Products must be unopened and in original packaging.

Eligible Returns

You can return products in the following cases:

  • Product is damaged or defective upon delivery
  • Product is expired or near expiry (less than 3 months)
  • Wrong product delivered (different from what you ordered)
  • Product packaging is tampered or open
  • Missing items from your order

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Opened or used medicines and healthcare products
  • Products without original packaging
  • Prescription medicines ordered correctly as per prescription
  • Personal care items that have been opened
  • Items marked as non-returnable at the time of purchase

Return Process

Step 1: Contact our customer support within 7 days of delivery

  • Phone: (021) 111-329-224
  • Email: pharmacy@dawaai.pk
  • WhatsApp: 0317-1719452

Step 2: Provide order details and reason for return

Step 3: Our team will verify your request and approve if eligible

Step 4: Return the product in original packaging with all accessories

Step 5: Refund will be processed within 7-10 business days

Refund Method

Refunds will be processed through the original payment method:

  • Online Payment: Refund to original payment source (7-10 business days)
  • Cash on Delivery: Bank transfer or store credit (as per preference)
  • Bank Cards: Refund to the original card used for payment

Exchange Policy

For wrong or damaged products, we offer direct exchange subject to product availability. The replacement will be delivered at no additional cost.

Cancellation Policy

Orders can be cancelled before dispatch without any charges. Contact customer support immediately if you wish to cancel your order.

After Dispatch: Once shipped, orders cannot be cancelled but can be refused at delivery for a full refund.

Delivery Issues

If you face any issues with delivery:

  • Delay in Delivery: Contact us if your order is delayed beyond the estimated time
  • Missing Delivery: Report within 24 hours if you don'''t receive your order
  • Damaged in Transit: Refuse acceptance and contact us immediately
