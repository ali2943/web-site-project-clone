Delivery Times

Major Cities (Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Rawalpindi): Orders placed before 5 PM are delivered within 24-48 hours.

Other Cities: Delivery takes 2-4 working days depending on location.

dShops Express Delivery (Karachi only): Get your medicines within 60 minutes of order confirmation.

Delivery Charges

Free Delivery: All orders above Rs. 1,000 qualify for free delivery across Pakistan.

Standard Delivery Fee: Rs. 99-150 for orders below Rs. 1,000 (varies by location).

Express Delivery (dShops): Rs. 150 flat fee for 60-minute delivery in Karachi.

Delivery Areas

We deliver to all major cities and most towns across Pakistan including:

Punjab: Lahore, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, Multan, Gujranwala, Sialkot, Bahawalpur, Sargodha

Sindh: Karachi, Hyderabad, Sukkur, Larkana, Mirpurkhas

KPK: Peshawar, Mardan, Abbottabad, Swat, Mingora

Balochistan: Quetta, Gwadar, Turbat

Other Areas: Islamabad, Muzaffarabad, Gilgit, Skardu

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is confirmed, you can track it in real-time through:

  • Dawaai mobile app
  • Website "Track Order" page
  • SMS updates at every stage
  • Email notifications

Contactless Delivery

For your safety, we offer contactless delivery options. Our delivery person will place your order at your doorstep and maintain social distancing. Payment can be made online to ensure a completely contactless experience.
