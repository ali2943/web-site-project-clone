Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 24-48 hours for major cities. Express delivery via dShops is available within 60 minutes in Karachi.

Can I change my delivery address?

Yes, you can change the delivery address before the order is dispatched. Please contact our customer support immediately.

What if I'm not available during delivery?

Our delivery partner will attempt to contact you. If unavailable, they will leave a notice and reschedule delivery.

How do I return an order?

Items can be returned within 7 days if damaged, expired, or incorrect. Please refer to our Refund Policy for details.
