Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Dawaai services

Ordering & Payment

How do I place an order on Dawaai?

You can place an order by searching for medicines on our website or app, adding them to your cart, and proceeding to checkout. If you have a prescription, you can upload it, and our pharmacists will create the order for you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods including Cash on Delivery (COD), Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard), Online Banking, JazzCash, EasyPaisa, and various bank cards with special discounts.

Do you offer any discounts or promotional codes?

Yes! We regularly offer discounts with various bank partners including HBL, Meezan Bank, Silk Bank, and Habib Metro Bank. We also have promotional voucher codes like "DawaaiDiscount" for up to 15% off. Check our Offers page for current promotions.

Is there a minimum order value?

There is no minimum order value for most cities. However, delivery charges may apply for orders below Rs. 1,000 in some areas. Check at checkout for specific details.

Delivery & Shipping

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 24-48 hours for major cities (Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad). For dShops service in Karachi, you can get your medicines delivered within 60 minutes! Delivery times may vary for remote areas.

What areas do you deliver to?

We deliver across Pakistan including major cities like Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, Multan, Peshawar, Quetta, and many more. Enter your address at checkout to confirm availability.

Are there any delivery charges?

Delivery is free for orders above Rs. 1,000 in most major cities. For orders below this amount, a nominal delivery fee of Rs. 99-150 may apply depending on your location.

Can I track my order?

Yes! You can track your order in real-time through our website or mobile app. You'll also receive SMS updates at every stage of delivery. Visit the "Track Order" page for more details.

Medicines & Products

Do I need a prescription to order medicines?

Prescription is required for all prescription medicines. You can upload your prescription during checkout, and our qualified pharmacists will verify it. Over-the-counter (OTC) medicines do not require a prescription.

Are the medicines authentic?

Absolutely! We guarantee 100% authentic medicines. All our products are sourced directly from authorized distributors and manufacturers. We are LegitScript Certified, an international certification that validates our commitment to quality and authenticity.

What if a medicine is out of stock?

If a medicine is out of stock, you can request to be notified when it becomes available. Our team will also contact you to suggest alternative medicines after consulting with our pharmacists.

Returns & Refunds

Can I return or exchange medicines?

Medicines can be returned within 7 days if the product is damaged, expired, or if you received the wrong item. Products must be unopened and in original packaging. Please refer to our Refund Policy page for detailed information.

How do I request a refund?

Contact our customer support team at (021) 111-329-224 or email pharmacy@dawaai.pk with your order details and reason for return. Our team will guide you through the refund process. Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days.

Doctor Consultation & Lab Tests

How do I book a doctor consultation?

You can book a doctor consultation through our website or mobile app. Choose your preferred doctor, select an available time slot, and make the payment. You'll receive a confirmation with details to join the video consultation.

How much does a doctor consultation cost?

Consultation fees vary by doctor specialty and experience, ranging from Rs. 500 to Rs. 2,500. We also offer free live chat with doctors for basic health queries.

Can I book lab tests for home sample collection?

Yes! We offer at-home sample collection services for various lab tests. Book your test online, and a phlebotomist will visit your home at your preferred time. Reports are delivered online within 24-48 hours.

General Questions

Is my personal and medical information secure?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. All personal and medical information is encrypted and stored securely. We follow strict confidentiality protocols and never share your data with third parties. Please read our Privacy Policy for more details.

Do you have a mobile app?

Yes! Our mobile app is available on both iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play Store). Download the app for a seamless experience, exclusive app-only offers, and easy access to all our services.
